Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Back to home

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Soft School ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when schools, administrators, staff, students, and parents use our school management platform.

2. Information we collect

To operate the platform, we may collect:

  • Account details for administrators, teachers, and staff, such as name, role, phone number, and email address.
  • Student records entered by the school, including name, class, attendance, and fee status.
  • Contact details for parents or guardians used for notifications and support.
  • Usage data such as login activity and device/browser information, used to keep the platform secure and reliable.

3. How we use information

Information collected through Soft School is used to operate core features such as student records, staff management, fees and finance, and attendance tracking; to provide customer support over WhatsApp or email; to send service-related notifications to schools, staff, and parents; and to maintain platform security and improve reliability.

4. Data ownership

Each school remains the owner of the student, staff, and fee data it enters into the platform. Soft School acts as a data processor on the school's behalf and does not sell or share this data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Limited information may be shared with trusted service providers (such as hosting or messaging providers) strictly to operate the platform, and these providers are bound to protect data confidentiality.

6. Data security

We use encryption in transit and at rest, restrict access to authorised roles only, and back up data regularly to reduce the risk of loss or unauthorised access.

7. Data retention

School data is retained for as long as the school's account is active, or as required by applicable record-keeping obligations. Schools may request deletion of their data by contacting us.

8. Your rights

Depending on your role and applicable local law, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data held about you. Requests can be directed to your school administrator or to us directly.

9. Children's data

Student records are entered and managed by the school on behalf of students and their parents or guardians. We rely on schools to obtain any necessary parental consent required under applicable law before entering student data into the platform.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Contact us

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to hello@softschool.com or via WhatsApp.
